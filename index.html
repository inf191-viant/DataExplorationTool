Welcome! MerQuery is an engaging user experience, effectively allowing you the ability to search through the digital warehouse of Viant's user profile data.


To use the system, just ensure you're logged into your Google BigQuery credentials through Specific Media. Just go ahead and plug information into the search fields on the right, select the number of results you want, and hit 'Enter'; we'll take care of the rest.


You can click through all hyperlinked fields and bring up individual user profiles when you see an icon next to the 'emailMD5' column entry. All search results can be exported out into an Excel file for later use. Enjoy!
